# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
VeriFact a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

Because this project touches politically charged territory (misinformation,
media credibility), we additionally pledge to keep discussion **evidence-based
and non-partisan**. Source-list debates are settled by citations to published
research, not by ideology.

## Our standards

Positive behaviour includes empathy, respect for differing viewpoints,
graceful acceptance of constructive criticism, and citing evidence.
Unacceptable behaviour includes trolling, insults, harassment, publishing
others' private information, and pushing source-list changes for partisan
reasons without evidence.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to
the maintainers via GitHub issues (or privately via the security contact in
SECURITY.md). All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
