This is the in-browser demo — it runs a subset of VeriFact entirely in your browser (source reputation, domain age, X-post extraction, writing-style & claim heuristics, news corroboration). The full engine adds article fetching, fact-check-database lookup, LLM claim analysis and media provenance — pip install git+https://github.com/AravindB98/verifact · docker compose up.
Nothing you paste leaves your browser except public lookups (RDAP, GDELT, FixTweet — and for Instagram/Facebook/LinkedIn links, a read-only public relay that sees only the URL).
